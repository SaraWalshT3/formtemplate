Baby Animals

They're so cute!

There was a study done at Hiroshima University in Japan by psychological research scientists that showed how looking at cute baby animal pictures can increase our productivity. They suggested this could be because looking at young animals is tied to nurturing feelings, which is tied to alertness. (Source linked on 'More Info' Page.)
